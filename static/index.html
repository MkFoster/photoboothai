PhotoBooth AI

A web photo booth that awards points for promoting charities on social media

Microsoft Azure AI Hackathon July 10 - September 10, 2019

Developer Circles Community Challenge July 9 - September 20, 2019


Note: This site uses your webcam to take pictures of you and upload them for analysis by AI services. Please click allow when prompted by your browser to use the webcam. Do you want to continue?

Status: Waiting for your first photo!

To test this out, download and printout the demo logo from below or click "Add logo sticker". The model is trained to recognize only this specific logo.

For this demo, we are going to pretend this is the logo for our favorite charity (since I don't have permission to use a real one). Hold the printed logo in view of the camera, smile big, and click "Take photo". Big smiles and logos earn big points and you will see a rectangle around your smiling face and the logo.

Want to challenge the logo recognition model? Printout some similar logos from https://placeholder.com/logos/ and see if PhotoboothAI boxes them and gives you credit or properly ignores them.

Thank you to the folks at https://placeholder.com for making these logos available for testing.
